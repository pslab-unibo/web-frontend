Best Student Paper Award at ACSOS 2025

  • Gianluca Aguzzi
  • Friday, Sep 12, 2025

I am thrilled to announce that our paper, “A Field-based Approach for Runtime Replanning in Swarm Robotics Missions”, won the Best Student Paper Award at ACSOS 2025!

In this work, we tackle the challenge of ensuring mission success for multi-robot systems in unpredictable environments. We propose a field-based runtime task replanning approach grounded in aggregate programming. This allows robots to make decentralized decisions and collectively adapt plans in real-time, even in areas with unreliable network infrastructure.

Our experiments show that this approach performs close to an oracle centralized planner and significantly outperforms baselines under sparse communication conditions.
