Project Emerge at European Researcher Night 2025!

  • Nicolas Farabegoli
  • Friday, Sep 12, 2025

We are excited to announce that Project Emerge, an open-source swarm robotics platform developed by our team, was showcased at the European Researcher Night 2025!

Project Emerge aims to democratize access to swarm robotics research by providing a comprehensive platform that includes both hardware and software components. The platform is designed to be modular, scalable, and easy to use, making it accessible to researchers, educators, and hobbyists alike. The project is completely open source, with all designs, code, and documentation available on GitHub: https://github.com/Project-Emerge

During the European Researcher Night, we had the opportunity to demonstrate the capabilities of Project Emerge to a diverse audience. Attendees were able to interact with the swarm robots, observe their collective behaviors, and learn about the underlying technologies that enable swarm intelligence. We would like to extend our gratitude to everyone who contributed to the success of Project Emerge, and in particular, the backers on Experiment.com: https://experiment.com/projects/project-emerge-an-open-source-swarm-robotics-platform
